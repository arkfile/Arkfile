Contrast Policy Summary

On Dark Surfaces (depth-1/2/3/4)

  • Primary text: salt
  • Secondary text: foam-1
  • Muted text: foam-2
  • Links/actions: current-2
  • Success: biolum
  • Warning: phosphor
  • Error: coral

On Semantic Backgrounds

  • biolum bg: depth-1 text
  • phosphor bg: depth-1 text
  • coral bg: salt text
  • current-1 bg: salt text

On Salt (White) Backgrounds

  • Use only: depth-1, depth-2, depth-3
  • Never use: foam-1, foam-2, current-*, salt

Borders / Dividers

  • Default: depth-4
  • Semantic: token-matched (coral, phosphor, biolum, current-2)
